2. Line Height (Leading)

.leading-none (1)

This text feels very cramped. When the line height is set to none, the descenders of one line might actually touch the ascenders of the line below it. It is rarely used for body paragraphs but works for massive, single-word titles.

.leading-relaxed (1.625)

This text has room to breathe. The "relaxed" setting is perfect for long-form reading, like blog posts or articles. It prevents the reader's eye from jumping to the wrong line accidentally and reduces eye strain significantly.
